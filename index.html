Welcome to GO CORONA GO!

A traditional 2 player infinite runner game based on real life!

Your goal is to travel as much as YOU CAN!

  1. Collect Vaccines to immune yourself against fake news!
  2. If you touch fake news/medicines, YOU WILL LOSE A LIFE!
  3. If you lose all lives YOU GET THE VIRUS!
  4. You can still run, with the virus and earn points...
  5. BUT IF YOU TOUCH YOUR FRIEND, THEN BOTH WILL HAVE THE VIRUS
  6. If both players get the virus, the game is over!
